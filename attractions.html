Top Attractions

Machu Picchu

Discover Peru’s iconic Inca citadel set high in the Andes, with unforgettable views and rich cultural history.

Sacred Valley

Explore traditional villages, colorful markets, and ancient ruins in one of Peru’s most scenic regions.

Cusco

Walk through cobblestone streets where Inca heritage and Spanish colonial architecture blend together.

Lake Titicaca

Visit the world’s highest navigable lake, known for striking scenery and unique island communities.

Nazca Lines

Witness Peru’s mysterious geoglyphs etched into the desert, visible from scenic flights above the plains.

Arequipa

Enjoy the “White City,” dramatic volcano views, and beautiful architecture built from volcanic stone.
